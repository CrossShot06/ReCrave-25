What is ReCrave?

The Students' Gymkhana of the National Institute of Technology Durgapur proudly presents the third edition of ReCrave — the Annual Food Fest of NIT Durgapur, to be held on the 8th and 9th of November, 2025. Over the years, Recrave has become one of the most anticipated campus events — a vibrant celebration of taste, creativity, and community that brings together students, food lovers, and local vendors.
